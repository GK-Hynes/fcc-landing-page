Research based teaching

Our teaching is based on the latest research into how your brain learns language. You won't sit and memorize abstract grammar rules. Everything will be in context. You'll be speaking English from day one. See this video for an idea of what we mean.

The Benefits of Bilingualism

Learning a second language has many benefits. You can do business on the international market. You can travel to new and exciting places. You can make new friends and learn about new cultures.

But learning a new language also changes how your brain works. Studies have shown that bilingual brains are stronger because managing two languages requires more mental exercise and keeps your brain healthy. See this video for an idea of what we mean.
